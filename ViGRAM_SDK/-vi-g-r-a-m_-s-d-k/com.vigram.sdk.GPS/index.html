A GPSDisconnectSignal is sent by a `GPSService` when a certain event occurs that should lead to the disconnection of a `GPSService`. Possible reasons include the unavailability of a connected peripheral, recording inactivity, issues with the connection to the correction task and/or configuration difficulties.
